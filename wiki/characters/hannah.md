# Hannah
*aka Hannah Half-Elven, Hannah the Forsaken — Servant of Osgilliath — Season 1 central antagonist*

## Overview
A half-elven woman born of an illicit union between the god [[Heimdall]] and Queen [[Aduni]] of House [[Thrandurassil]]. Rejected by elven society for her mixed blood, Hannah was cast out to live as a farmer's daughter on the outer edge of [[Dres Elas]]. She grew into a violent, cunning figure who eventually bargained with the demon lord [[Osgilliath]] — giving herself to his power in a blood ritual beneath an old pagan temple.

She emerged from that ritual transformed: her arms remade as fiendish claws, her followers reborn as Blood Elves. Hannah the Forsaken became the head of a growing fiendish cult. The Elves ultimately imprisoned her soul in a magical demi-plane portal; the party freed her in [[1.2.0 - A city overrun]] by shattering the ruby keystoning the portal.

Her corporeal resurrection is the central goal of [[The Chosen]].

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

Hannah is a demigod — daughter of Heimdall, who is a god. This makes her soul extremely powerful and difficult to permanently destroy. The Elves had imprisoned her rather than killed her precisely because she could not be killed outright.

The Chosen's resurrection ritual requires a body of sufficient power — a vessel from Heimdall's bloodline. Current candidate: **Ygritte** (stolen bride of Balder, now wife of Ivar). This is the "Blood Elves abduct one of Heimdall's line" beat from the Act 1 plot outline.

Osgilliath is a Pit Lord, lord of the 3rd tier of Hel.

## DM Notes — Family Tree Addition
> Private.

The Thrandurassil family tree mural (session 1.6.1/1.6.2) reveals:
- Thrandurassil bloodline descends from **Odin**
- Hannah's lineage: Odin → Cyrandel → Aduni + Hadran → *Hannah* (and Caerdan)
- Hannah has divine lineage on **both sides**: Odin (mother's Thrandurassil line) and Heimdall (father)
- Her entry was concealed with magic on the mural — the Speaker maintained the concealment
- Her sarcophagus in the Thrandurassil tombs was **empty** — corpse already taken/used by the Chosen

This double divine heritage makes Hannah significantly more powerful than a simple Osgilliath-blessed fiend. Her soul is a convergence of two divine bloodlines.
