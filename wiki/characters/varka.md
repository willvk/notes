# Varka
*aka "Low Tide" Varka Brogg — Proprietor of [[The Black Tide Tavern]] — Half-Orc, Female, Mid-40s*

## Overview
A former pirate who lost her ship and crew in a naval ambush. Retired to [[Mongeet]] and took over the Black Tide Tavern. Deep scars across her arms, a missing tusk. Commands respect with a booming voice and iron glare. Allows smugglers to operate under her roof under three rules: no killing, no cheating the house, no slitting throats where she drinks.

One of the three founding Fingers of [[Loki's Hand]] — alongside Kvarrac and Brelka. She and Kvarrac found Brelka in the smuggling caves and built the Hand together. She is now the only surviving founder.

During Brelka's coup at the Tavern (session 1.2.4), Varka killed five wererat attackers including **Grettir**, Brelka's lieutenant — while the party defended the room.

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

Varka still owes a debt to an unknown benefactor who helped her escape execution. Strange individuals occasionally arrive to collect on it — she has no choice but to comply.

The Black Tide Tavern has three hidden secrets:
1. **Ghost Ship Signal** — on foggy nights the tavern's lanterns flicker in a specific pattern; a call for smugglers to meet at the docks. The tavern is used as a signal post for illicit trade.
2. **River Monster's Tribute** — every full moon a mysterious offering appears at the base of the stilts (bloody fish, trinkets, coins). Something beneath the river is being appeased.
3. **Hidden Trapdoor** — behind the bar under a keg, a trapdoor leads to an underground cavern that goes much deeper than storage.
