# Harald
*Claimed son of Ivar — biological son of Balder — now de facto Norse leader*

## Overview
A young man (was 13 when introduced, now approaching adulthood). Raised as Ivar's son. Witnessed the Speaker's reveal and the Blood Elf attack on the council chambers from the stairs (session 1.7.1 is written partly from his POV). Present at the council when the chaos broke.

Following the events of session 1.7.3, Balder has joined Harald's retinue as Steward, helping to hold the Norse tribes together.

## Plot Involvement
- 1.5.1: Witnessed Ivar strike Ygritte; witnessed Ygritte's abduction; heard the Speaker's monologue
- 1.7.1: POV character during the Blood Elf attack. Watched Balder spare Ivar at sword point ("I am not a strong enough man"). Immediately drew his own blade and ran Ivar through from behind. His words: *"You are NOT my father."* — spoken not knowing it is literally true. Session cliffhanger.
- 1.7.3: Balder joins his retinue as Steward; Harald now leads the Norse tribes

## DM Notes
> Private.

Harald is Balder's biological son. Ivar was infertile, stole Ygritte from Balder, and raised Harald as his own (see [[characters/ivar|Ivar]]). Balder believes his son is dead (a farmhand's stillborn was used as the decoy). Neither Balder nor Harald knows the truth.

This is a slow-burn arc. Balder is now literally serving his own son as Steward without knowing it. Harald has Balder's blood but Ivar's upbringing. The truth emerging would be character-defining for both.

Harald killed Ivar himself — stabbing him through the heart in pity and disgust at the end of the Blood Elf assault. He saw the ruin of a man Ivar had become, with Ygritte just stolen, and chose to end it. He doesn't know Ivar wasn't his biological father. He now leads the Norse tribes, with Balder as Steward — literally being served by the father he doesn't know he has.

## Contradictions & Open Questions
- ✅ Ivar confirmed dead — killed by Harald, end of 1.7.1
- ❓ Does Harald have any combat capability? He's been largely background but clearly capable enough to kill a declining Ivar.
- ❓ When does the Balder/Harald truth emerge? This needs to be planned deliberately.
