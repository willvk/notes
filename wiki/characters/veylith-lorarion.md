# Veylith Lorarion
*aka "Voice Behind the Veil" — Elven diplomat, Loki's Hand contact, leader of the elven rebellion*

## Overview
A master glassblower and hidden rebel leader of the Western Elf Shallows. Loki's Hand's primary elven contact, brought into the Hand by Kvarrac as part of a food-for-secrets arrangement. Was captured by the Uzzikh (elite Ork warband) in session 1.2.2 during their investigation into the disappearance of Rashan. Rescued by the party in session 1.2.3.

Became the diplomatic face and leader of the elven uprising in sessions 1.4.04–1.4.10. Addressed the Pact directly in session 1.5.1. Has grown into a significant political ally.
