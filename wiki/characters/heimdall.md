# Heimdall
*aka The Ever-Watchful, Guardian of the End, Keeper of the Silent Vigil — God of Vigilance, Duty, and the Last Watch*

## Overview
Alignment: Lawful Neutral. Favoured weapon: Glaive. Domains: Duty, Protection, Perception, Fate.

The tireless sentinel who stands at the edge of fate, watching from his fortress **Gjallarborg** at the threshold between the mortal world and the unknown. His eyes pierce the veil of time; his ears hear a blade unsheathed from a hundred leagues away.

In Valkair, Heimdall is revered by guardians, rangers, sentinels, and seers. His symbol is the **Golden Eye**. The sound of a distant horn is an omen — either warning of great peril or a call to an unbreakable stand.

Edicts: Remain ever watchful, fulfill your duty no matter the cost, stand as the last line of defense, uncover hidden truths.
Anathema: Neglect a sacred duty, allow a threat to pass unnoticed, turn away from a sworn oath.

## Role in Valkair (in person)
Heimdall appears in the Shallows when he senses Hannah's presence re-entering the world after her release from the Elven prison. He presents himself as a loud, charismatic, boisterous wanderer — calling himself simply "a wanderer" unless pressed. He says something has drawn him to this place again — a feeling he hasn't felt in a long time.

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

Heimdall is Hannah's father. He had an affair with Queen Aduni of House Thrandurassil when he visited her court as a diplomat from the northern reaches. He was banished back to his people before he ever knew Aduni was pregnant. He has **no knowledge that Hannah exists**.

He is drawn to the Shallows because Hannah's soul has been freed and he feels the pull of his own blood. He does not understand why yet.

**Heimdall's Bloodline:** Mirroring his role in Norse myth as the progenitor of the caste system, Heimdall travelled widely across Valkair and fathered children among many peoples. His bloodline is scattered broadly across social classes and races. [[Ygritte]] is a confirmed carrier. [[Hannah]] is his most powerful known descendant (demigod, further enhanced by Osgilliath). There are likely many more. See [[lore/heimdalls-bloodline|Heimdall's Bloodline]] for full detail.

The degree of power the bloodline confers on diluted descendants is undefined — room to develop as needed.
