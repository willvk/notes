# Player Characters

## Brawn
*Orc — Fighter/Monk — Guild backstory*

From Murgrog, an orc village known for grappling and riding large animals as mounts. Was bribed and then set up by [[The Guild]] (via [[Tarod]]). Jailed for 5 years. In the deep quiet of imprisonment he found monk ways. Came to the Shallows for a fresh start, avoiding the law.

**1.7.5 — Tyr's Mark:** Sacrificed his hand in the Temple of Tyr. His right hand was replaced by living chain-link — pale silver, warm, fully functional. Bears a grey scale tattoo of Tyr on the back of his wrist.

*Chain Limb:* Unarmed Strike (1d8 bludgeoning, Grapple trait; can Grapple without a free hand). Once per day: on a successful unarmed Strike or Grapple with the chain limb, target makes DC [Brawn's class DC] Fortitude or becomes Restrained until end of his next turn. DC scales with class DC — intentional design.

## Gunnar
*Half-Orc (orc father, elven mother) — Ranger/Fighter — Pack backstory*

Born of rape — orc father was a member of [[The Pack]], which raided elven encampments. His elven mother sacrificed herself for him despite this. Found as a baby by The Pack; grew up raiding with them. Possesses a mirror that connects him to the supernatural — part of a burial tomb ritual covered in runes for Odin and Hel. His only belonging from infancy. In Hammerfall as a merc/bounty hunter.

## Hanzt
*Human — Alchemist/Rogue — Guild backstory*

Street kid from [[Yorvik]], taken in by [[The Guild]]. Known within the Guild as "Pathogen" — an alchemical interrogator and enforcer. Worked with other Guild enforcers including [[Tarod]]. Was tricked into interrogating an innocent family; attacked the Guild and freed them. Now on the run. Wants to atone; wants to be considered good. Came to the Shallows having changed his appearance considerably.

## Riven
*Unknown — Ranger — Orphanage backstory*

The orphanage he grew up in was a Guild slave farm. Three friends — Liora (gave him an amulet), Torran (protector), and Etta (gave him a bow blessed by Ullr) — saved all the kids, sacrificing themselves. Riven wants to find his three friends. The magic bow somehow helps him do this. Gods are opposed to the Elves.

**1.7.5 — Tyr's Mark:** Sacrificed Winter's Vigil (Etta's bow) in the Temple of Tyr — his most significant possession, given by someone who gave their life for him. Tyr returned a bow: hardwood, exquisitely made, runes covering its full length. Currently unidentified — presenting as a Godsbreath shortbow. Bears a grey scale tattoo of Tyr on the back of his wrist.

*Rune Bow (unidentified):* True nature is a Tyr artifact. Design pending — see [[plot/sessions/1.7.5|Session 1.7.5]] DM Notes for design direction. Reveal pacing: features should surface through use and narrative moments, not Identify spells.

## Balder
*Human — Norse — Yorvik backstory (non-core PC)*

Small, self-reliant. Fled [[Yorvik]] after the death of the sun. Former Jarl of Yorvik. [[Ivar]] joined a raid with him, then stole his wife [[Ygritte]] and murdered Balder's son. (DM truth: the murdered son was a farmhand's stillborn — Harald is actually Balder's biological son, being raised by Ivar.) Alcoholic. Working at [[The Black Tide Tavern]] for money.

## Eirunn
*Bear — Riven's animal companion*

A bear, not a PC. Part of Riven's backstory. Has not been significantly utilised in Act 1. Potential vehicle for Riven character development in Act 2 if needed.
