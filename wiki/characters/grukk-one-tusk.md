# Grukk One-Tusk
*Elite Ork guard — Horde — Recurring ally*

## Overview
A towering, veteran Ork. One tusk cracked and snapped halfway down; eyes sharp with years of judgment. Speaks in gravel. Commands respect through a lifetime of practical violence. First encountered in session 1.3.2 when he led six elite Horde guards into the catacombs and found the party amid the aftermath of the Barghest fight and the elven corpses.

Chose not to arrest the party — recognised they were victims of the same events, not perpetrators. Let them go via the stairs to the Eastern Elf Shallows.

## Plot Involvement
- 1.3.2: Found party in the catacombs; chose to release them rather than drag them to the Pact in chains
- 1.6.3: Discovered two runes while securing the catacombs after investigating the fallen Ork guard; provided them to the party
- 1.7.3: Listed among survivors at the council chambers after the Blood Elf attack

## DM Notes
> Private.

Grukk's distrust of the Chosen was visible even before their reveal — he added an aside to his guards about not caring why the Chosen claimed the temples. This makes him a potentially useful contact now that the Chosen are exposed.

He found 2 of the 8 Pilgrimage runes while doing his own investigation into the catacombs. He handed them over, which was an act of practical cooperation.
