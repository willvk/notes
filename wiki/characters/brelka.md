# Brelka
*aka "The Riptide" — Loki's Hand Finger — Human, Female, 40s — **Deceased (killed by party, session 1.2.8)***

## Overview
A sailor-turned-smuggler who dealt in rare sea-based contraband — banned elixirs, hallucinogenic seaweed. One of Kvarrac's Fingers in [[Loki's Hand]]. Served as the Catalyst boss confronting the party before being killed in session 1.2.8.

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

Brelka contracted **lycanthropy (wererat)** on a smuggling job. The disease drove her paranoid and increasingly violent.

She also found **[[lore/lokis-kiss|Loki's Kiss]]** — a cursed ring. Loki's influence flowing through it compounded the lycanthropy and drove her to betray Kvarrac and Loki's Hand. The dark irony: Loki used his own Hand to tear itself apart.

She spread the lycanthropy to trusted associates. The full scope of who was infected is unresolved — there may be remaining wererats in the Shallows.

Gunnar took Loki's Kiss from her body. He is now the ring's wearer.
