# Ivar
*aka Ivar the Terrible, Ivar Varangr — Jarl, Pact representative for the Norsemen*

## Overview
Leader of the Norsemen who took [[Hammerfall]] from the [[Elves]]. United the various Norse tribes north of Hammerfall into the largest human host in recorded history, then allied with [[Orgrim]] and his Orkish tribes to form the Great Heathen Army. Successfully conquered Hammerfall. Now sits on [[The Pact]] representing Norsemen interests.

Pact duties: Law, food allocation, property, trials and punishment, maintenance, management of the Elven population, management of Elven artifacts and magics.

## Plot Involvement
- Balder's backstory: Ivar joined a raid with Balder, stole Ygritte, murdered Balder's son
- Struck Ygritte in the council chambers (session 1.5.1) — Harald witnessed it
- The Speaker looked pointedly at Ivar when revealing "a new host has been found" — taunting him that his wife was being taken
- Ygritte abducted by Blood Elves during the chaos immediately after
- Session 1.7.1: **Killed by Harald** — cliffhanger ending. Balder had Ivar at sword point and chose to spare him ("I am not a strong enough man"). As Balder turned away, Harald drew his blade and ran Ivar through from behind. *"You are NOT my father."* Harald does not know this is literally true.
- Session 1.7.3: Loot distributed: "+2 weapon potency from Ivar" and "resilient rune from Ivar." Balder joins Harald's retinue as Steward to hold the Norse tribes together.

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

Ivar is infertile. He could not father his own children, so he stole Balder's wife Ygritte and claimed Balder's 13-year-old son **Harald** as his own. The corpse staked to a pyre and sailed down the river to [[Yorvik]] was a farmhand's stillborn — used to quash rumours of Ivar's shame and erase Balder's claim as the father.

Harald is Balder's biological son. Ivar raised him as his own. This is the Oedipus-adjacent secret at the heart of Balder's arc.
