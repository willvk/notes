# Caerdan Thrandurassil
*Current High King of the Elves — Hannah's half-brother*

## Overview
The current High King of the Elves. Of House Thrandurassil. Has a statue to himself in Hammerfall. Legitimate son of Queen Aduni and King Hadran; raised to the throne through the established line.

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

**The Family Tree (revealed in session 1.6.2, Tomb of the Lost):**

```
Odin
  └─ Thrandurassil (all 5 elven houses)
       └─ Cyrandel (Elaria)
            ├─ Aduni — married Hadran
            │    ├─ Caerdan Thrandurassil (legitimate heir, current High King)
            │    └─ *Hannah* (concealed — entry hidden by magic on the mural)
            └─ Laeroth
```

Hannah's entry on the family mural in the Thrandurassil tombs was concealed with magic. The Speaker knew it should be there — he seemed to be aware of Hannah's suppressed lineage and had something to do with maintaining the concealment.

Caerdan is Hannah's half-brother. He is the legitimate heir; she is the erased one. Whether Caerdan knows Hannah exists is unknown.

The Thrandurassil bloodline traces back to Odin — which makes Hannah a divine descendant on both sides (Odin through her mother's line, Heimdall through her father).
