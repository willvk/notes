# Kvarrac
*aka The Rat King — Former leader of [[Loki's Hand]] — Smallfolk (Ratfolk), aged — **Deceased (killed by Brelka, session 1.2.4)***

## Overview
An aged, wiry ratfolk dressed in finery. Soft-spoken, walked with a staff he didn't seem to need. Personality was unpredictable — switched from charming to murderous in an instant. Led [[Loki's Hand]] and controlled [[Mongeet]]'s criminal underworld until Brelka drove a dagger into his chest repeatedly in the backroom of [[The Black Tide Tavern]].

Brelka's speech before the killing made clear their history: she found Kvarrac half-starved and hunted in the smuggling caves, with the Guild, the Chosen, and even the Elves wanting him dead. She, Varka, and Kvarrac formed the original three Fingers of the Hand. She killed him for going soft — feeding elves and orphans instead of keeping Mongeet brutal and feared.

- Killed by Brelka in the Black Tide Tavern backroom during her coup (session 1.2.4)

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

Kvarrac rules the **Undercroft** — a subsection of old tunnels turned smuggler's haven beneath Mongeet. His enemies tend to vanish; their bones wash up weeks later.

He has a deep hatred for the city's upper elite and has historically stolen from them — but recently he's gone quiet, prioritizing building a network of allies in the Shallows by smuggling in food and selling it cheap through specific distributors. Brelka interprets this as weakness and going soft.

The Guild has bribed one of the Fingers (Loki's Hand lieutenants) to undermine him. This is the invisible hand behind Brelka's discontent, though Brelka believes her motivations are her own.

## Contradictions & Open Questions
- ❓ Loki's Hand leadership vacuum — both Kvarrac and Brelka are now dead. Who leads the Hand? Varka was one of the original three Fingers; does she step up, or does someone else fill the void?
