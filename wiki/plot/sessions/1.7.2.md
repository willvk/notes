# Session 1.7.2 — "Her Speaker"

*Act 1 — Hammerfall*

## Summary

The council chamber siege continues from 1.7.1. The party holds against waves of Blood Elves and demonic creatures as the night drags on. The session's central beat: the party confronts and kills **The Speaker**, silencing the public face of the Chosen. The session ends with dawn breaking, the attackers retreating, and Hammerfall surviving its first night of assault — battered but alive.

**Cliffhanger carry-in from 1.7.1:** Harald has just stabbed Ivar through the heart.

## Key Events

### The Night-Long Siege
The Blood Elf assault (begun in 1.7.1) is sustained through the night. All major factions trapped in the council chambers fight their own corner:
- The party holds the main floor
- Orgrim's Orks defend their cluster
- Harald and the Norse remnants hold near Ivar's fallen body
- Grukk One-Tusk survives — one of the few Ork elites left standing
- Yennen (Guild) present but non-combatant

The humans were in trouble throughout — "Ivar not the fighter he once was" proved an understatement. With Ivar dead, Harald has no choice but to step up.

### The Speaker Confronted
The Speaker — revealed in 1.7.1 as the public face of the Chosen — is present in the council chambers. He does not flee. He is confident, fanatical, and expects his creatures to overwhelm. The party fights through to him.

His final words confirm the full scope of the threat:
- Hannah will rise in **5 nights** at midnight
- The Chosen have everything they need — a vessel (Ygritte, Heimdall's bloodline) has been taken
- Nightly attacks will continue; the catacombs are their domain

The party kills him. **500gp reward** for the kill.

### Harald and the Norse Aftermath
Harald stands over Ivar's body. He killed his claimed father — the man who took his mother, who drank away his strength, who sat heavy in his chair while the world burned. He does not know Ivar was not his biological father. The grief and relief are both real.

With Ivar dead, Harald becomes the de facto Norse representative. He is young, inexperienced, but Balder is there.

### Dawn
At dawn the creatures withdraw, retreating back into the catacombs. The survivors emerge from barricades across Hammerfall. The city held — for now.

## End State
- **The Speaker is dead.** The Chosen's human face is gone; their plans are exposed.
- **Ivar is dead.** Harald now leads the Norse tribes.
- **Ygritte is taken.** Held in the catacombs as the resurrection vessel.
- **5 nights until Hannah's resurrection ritual.**
- Party advances to **level 7.**
- Balder joins Harald's retinue as his Steward.
- The city knows something terrible is happening. The party knows exactly what.
