## Tyr Sacrifice Mechanic

The Fenrir golem's jaw opens on death, revealing the Tyr Rune. The Elvish inscription reads: *"What do you give willingly, that cannot be returned?"*

Tiered voluntary sacrifice:
- **Minor** — cosmetic grey scale on the hand, no mechanical reward
- **Significant** — **Tyr's Binding**: once/day, on a successful grapple the target makes DC 15 Will or becomes frightened
- **Extreme (oath)** — **Tyr's Favour**: advantage vs. compulsion/domination/fear while oath holds; scale cracks if broken
- **Extreme (body part)** — living chain-link replacement, counts as 1d8 weapon; once/long rest: DC 16 Fortitude or Restrained 1 round on touch

> A sworn Tyr oath on Gunnar creates direct tension with Loki's Kiss — flag for future escalation.

## Depth 4 — World Design

**Architecture:** Partially constructed elven work giving way to natural cave. The elves abandoned this level when Hannah became the Forsaken (~500 years ago). Never completed.

**The Kobolds:** A tribe displaced by the elves 500 years ago. When the elves withdrew, the kobolds expanded into the abandoned depth and have been here ever since. Engineering and completion-focused culture — they have been trying to finish the unfinished elven temples for generations without knowing what the temples are for. First representation of kobolds in Valkair.

**Key NPCs:**
- **Vress** — matriarch, old, grey-scaled, one milky eye, three fingers missing. Speaks only old dialect/Draconic.
- **Skit** — young interpreter at Vress's shoulder. Primary party contact going forward.

**The Sakapatate:** Multi-pilot kobold war construct. The primary one is kept at the red corridor entrance as their ultimate defense.

**The Red Corridor:** Connects Depth 4 to the Upper Hammerfall catacombs. Sealed by the kobolds ~200 years ago when the Chosen's horrors started coming through. Elven drainage channels run floor-to-midwall with centuries of dried blood. Sound of dripping — something still flows, and has increased lately (Hannah's resurrection preparations = more victims above = warmer blood). The kobolds have had 3 taken in the last month.

**The Three Shrines:** Odin, Thor, and the sealed Freya shrine. The kobolds have been living around them for 400 years, trying to "complete" them without knowing their purpose.

**The Freya Shrine:** Sealed from this side — the kobolds cannot open it. Accessible via teleportation circle at The Hollow Coin (Richeri). Vress has objects recovered from near its entrance over the years — potential Hollow Coin hook items.
