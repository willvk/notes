# Act 1 Finale — The Return of Hannah

*The party enters the Chosen's sanctum with all 8 runes. The ritual has already completed. Ygritte is dead. Hannah the Forsaken is rising — but Hannah Half-Elven is fighting back from inside.*

---

## Setup — The Twist

**The runes were not a key. They were fuel.**

The Pilgrimage of the Eight was originally an elven consecration rite — completing the temples released each god's divine essence outward through the catacomb network toward a central point. Osgilliath subverted this. The Chosen removed the runes to prevent uncontrolled flow, then relied on the party to collect and return them at the right moment.

When the party seats the eighth rune and the door opens: the ritual completes. Not almost — completes.

**Fragment V** ("The soul is not to interfere, the Forsaken will be reborn") was the Speaker's insurance note. The soul IS interfering. The party's job is to let it.

---

## The Sanctum

The room is built around a black stone altar — Ygritte's body is there, already dead. Root-veins of Osgilliath's corruption spread from the altar across floor and walls, pulsing with each of Hannah's actions.

The root-veins are environmental hazards: difficult terrain, and periodic forced saves against corruption effects. They track Hannah's state visually — strong in Phase 1, cracking in Phase 2, splitting and erupting in Phase 3. By Phase 3 the floor is compromised and the ceiling is coming down.

## Boss Fight — HANNAH THE FORSAKEN

> *Unique, Medium, Demon, Fiend*
>
> **Perception** +21; darkvision, tremorsense (imprecise) 30 ft.
> **Languages** Common, Elvish, Abyssal
> **Skills** Athletics +24, Intimidation +22, Deception +19
> **Str** +7, **Dex** +3, **Con** +5, **Int** +3, **Wis** +5, **Cha** +5
>
> **AC** 30; **Fort** +22, **Ref** +17, **Will** +24
> **HP** 240; **Immunities** fear; **Weaknesses** holy 10; **Resistances** physical 10 (except cold iron)
>
> **Forsaken Presence** *(Aura 30 ft., Emotion, Fear, Mental)* Each non-fiend creature that enters or begins its turn in the aura must attempt a **DC 30 Will save**. Failure: **Frightened 2**. Critical failure: **Frightened 3** and **Fleeing** until end of next turn. Success or critical success: immune to this aura for 24 hours.
>
> ---
>
> **Speed** 30 feet, climb 20 feet
>
> **Fiendish Claws** *(Strike)* +22/+17/+12; **Damage** 2d10+13 slashing; applies **Blood Mark** on any hit; applies **Grab** as a free action on any hit.
>
> **Rend** *(One Action)* Requirement: one creature within reach is Grabbed. Makes a Fiendish Claws Strike against that creature. On a hit, target also takes **2d6 persistent bleed damage**.
>
> **Blood Mark** *(Passive)* Black demon blood coats the target — visible to everyone. A Blood-Marked creature may spend **1 action** to wipe it off (no roll). Also applied to any creature failing its save against Corrupted Blood Spray. Blood-Marked creatures are the only valid targets for Demonic Possession and Nightmare Manifestation.

### Phase 2 — Unlocked after first Hannah window *(at 160 HP)*

> **Corrupted Blood Spray** *(Two Actions, Poison)* Hannah opens her own veins. 30-foot cone. **DC 30 Fortitude.**
> — Critical success: No effect.
> — Success: 4d8 poison damage; Blood-Marked.
> — Failure: 4d8 poison damage; Blood-Marked; **Sickened 1**.
> — Critical failure: 8d8 poison damage; Blood-Marked; **Sickened 2**.
> *Blood persists as difficult terrain until end of encounter.*
>
> **Demonic Possession** *(Two Actions, Enchantment, Incapacitation, Mental)* Requirement: one Blood-Marked creature within 30 feet. **DC 30 Will.**
> — Critical success: Immune to Demonic Possession for 24 hours.
> — Success: No effect.
> — Failure: **Confused** for 1 round.
> — Critical failure: **Controlled** for 1 round — Hannah dictates one action on her next turn.

---

### Phase 3 — Unlocked after second Hannah window *(at 80 HP)*

> **Nightmare Manifestation** *(Two Actions, Emotion, Fear, Incapacitation, Mental)* Requirement: one Blood-Marked creature within 30 feet. **DC 32 Will.**
> — Critical success: Immune for 24 hours.
> — Success: **Frightened 2**.
> — Failure: **Stunned 1** and **Frightened 2**.
> — Critical failure: **Stunned 2** and **Frightened 3**.
>
> **Thrashing Desperation** *(Reaction)* Trigger: Hannah takes 20 or more damage from a single hit. Effect: She immediately makes a Fiendish Claws Strike against the attacker.
>
> **Corruption Burst** *(Replaces Corrupted Blood Spray)* Now a **20-foot burst centred on Hannah** rather than a directed cone. Same DC and effects. She has lost control of it.

## Phase Transitions & Hannah Windows

| HP Threshold | Event |
|---|---|
| 160 HP | **Hannah window 1** — out of initiative, see below. Phase 2 unlocks. |
| 80 HP | **Hannah window 2** — shorter, she's losing. Phase 3 unlocks. |
| 25 HP | **Final window** — she asks. Party answers. |
| 0 HP | She finishes it. The Forsaken dies. So does she. |

### Window 1 — at 160 HP

*Read aloud when triggered:*

The corruption visibly pulls back. The thing before you stills — then Hannah Half-Elven is standing there, looking at her hands, looking at you. She's confused. Urgent. Aware.

> *"I remember everything. Both times. I know what it wants."*
> *"It can't lock me out completely. Not yet. But it's close."*
> *(She looks at Ygritte's body.)*
> *"I'm sorry about her. I couldn't stop it."*

She can answer 1–2 questions. Then the corruption surges. She fights it — visibly. She loses. The Forsaken comes back angry.

*Phase 2 abilities are now active.*

### Window 2 — at 80 HP

*Shorter. She barely made it.*

> *"It's almost locked me out. I can feel the door closing."*
> *"There's a way to end this. I know how. But you have to let me."*
> *(She doesn't explain yet — she's running out of time.)*
> *"Keep fighting. One more time. Give me one more moment."*

Then she's gone. Faster than before. The Forsaken comes back harder and it knows she's been talking to them.

*Phase 3 abilities are now active. Corruption Burst replaces Corrupted Blood Spray.*

### Final Window — at 25 HP

*She erupts through. This costs everything she has left. She won't get another chance.*

> *"I'm Heimdall's daughter. The Forsaken feeds on Osgilliath's bargain — but I made that bargain. I can unmake it from the inside. Turn myself into the thing that breaks it."*
> *"It kills us both. But it's done. Properly done."*
> *(She looks at each of them.)*
> *"I've had five hundred years to think about what I became. I don't want to be saved. I want to choose how this ends."*
> *"Will you let me?"*

**The party must answer.** Each PC gets a moment if they want it. This is the vote.

If they say yes: combat resumes. The Forsaken is now fighting on two fronts — the party outside, Hannah inside. It is losing ground on both. The final HP is the party holding on while Hannah does the internal work. When it hits 0: **Hannah finishes it, not the party.**

---

## Resolution

The corruption collapses. The root-veins go dark. The room stops falling.

Hannah Half-Elven is there — free for a moment, in control of nothing. She is not Ygritte. She is Hannah, and she knows it, and she is at peace for the first time in five hundred years.

**Heimdall appears.** He has been watching. He does not intervene. He witnesses.

She looks at him. Whatever passes between them is brief.

She looks at the party.

Then she lets go.
