# The Pilgrimage of the Eight
*Collect all 8 divine runes to open the sealed door to the Chosen's sanctuary — 5 nights remaining*

## Status
**Active — URGENT.** 5/8 runes collected. The resurrection ritual begins at midnight in 5 days (from session 1.7.3). Tyr rune claimed in 1.7.5.

## What the Players Know
- A vast door carved with the likenesses of the Eight Gods blocks the way to the Chosen's sanctuary
- Each god's symbol was once inset with a divine rune — all hollow when found
- Veylith identified it as the Pilgrimage of the Eight — an ancient elven rite through 8 temples beneath the Shallows
- They must collect all 8 runes to open the door
- 4 runes collected so far
- Plans found on the Speaker confirm: resurrection ritual in 5 nights at midnight

## What They Don't Know (DM)
- The door leads to wherever Ygritte is being held and where the resurrection ritual will take place
- Whether disrupting the rune collection by the Chosen has delayed or changed the ritual timeline
