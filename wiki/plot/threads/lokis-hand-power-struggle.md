# Loki's Hand Power Struggle
*Brelka built a coup against Kvarrac — driven by Loki's Kiss and wererat lycanthropy — resolved when the party killed her in session 1.2.8*

## Status
**Resolved** — Brelka killed by party (session 1.2.8). Immediate coup threat ended. Residual: wererat infections in the Shallows may remain unresolved.

## What the Players Know
- Brelka was a Finger in Loki's Hand who went wrong — they killed her
- She was a wererat and had infected others
- She had Loki's Kiss, a cursed ring now worn by Gunnar
- Kvarrac still leads Loki's Hand

## What They Don't Know (DM)
- Loki's Kiss was the primary driver of Brelka's betrayal — Loki subverted his own Hand deliberately
- The Guild had bribed Brelka as their Finger in Loki's Hand; their destabilisation effort died with her
- Brelka infected an unknown number of associates — there may be wererats still in the Shallows
- Kvarrac's food smuggling operation was strategic, not weakness

## Key Players
- [[Kvarrac]] — target; cautious, building allies
- [[Brelka]] — instigator; wererat, disease-driven
- [[The Guild]] — invisible hand; bribed a Finger
- Unnamed bribed Finger — identity TBD

## Key Locations
- [[Mongeet]] — Loki's Hand territory
- The Undercroft — Kvarrac's base beneath Mongeet
- [[The Black Tide Tavern]] — adjacent; Varka is aware of Loki's Hand operations

## Session History
- **1.2.4** "Over Claw and Tide": Brelka and Grettir marched into the Black Tide Tavern backroom and confronted Kvarrac. Brelka gave a speech about his betrayal of the Hand's original purpose, then stabbed him to death. She and her wererats changed forms and attacked. Varka killed five attackers including Grettir. Brelka escaped. Kvarrac is dead.
- **1.2.8** "Loki's Kiss": Party killed Brelka. Gunnar took Loki's Kiss from her body.
