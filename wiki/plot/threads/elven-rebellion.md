# The Elven Rebellion
*The Shallows elves rose up; party sided with them; partial victory achieved before Blood Elves interrupted*

## Status
**Resolved** — rebellion concluded when Blood Elves erupted from a Hel rift during the final battle (session 1.4.11). Both sides turned against the common threat. Partial elven demands met.

## What the Players Know
- The party sided with the elves, fought across a war map of the Shallows
- Key elven commanders rescued and worked with: Aelion Varethil, Theren Silquar, Vaelen Duskthorn, Veylith Lorarion
- Pact's extermination response was brutal — suicide bombers (rune-carved flesh), direct military assault
- Party used the Sentinel Horn to create tactical diversions
- Elves successfully took key hex positions, summoned ancient elven spirits at Heimdall's Temple to wipe Pact forces
- Blood Elves erupted from a Hel rift mid-battle — Pact and Elves briefly united against them
- Elven demands delivered to the Pact (1.5.1): run own businesses, travel within Shallows, carry money

## What They Don't Know (DM)
- The Hel rift was a Chosen escalation — deliberate or incidental to the timing?
- Whether the Pact will honour the elven demands now that the Chosen crisis has overtaken everything
