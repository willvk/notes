# Hannah's Resurrection
*The Chosen are working to restore Hannah the Forsaken to a corporeal form*

## Status
**Active — CRITICAL.** The Speaker is dead. The Chosen are revealed. Ygritte is taken. **5 nights remain until the resurrection ritual at midnight.** Last touched: session 1.7.3.

## What the Players Know
- Hannah the Forsaken is a demigod servant of Osgilliath, daughter of Heimdall — Heimdall told the party (session 1.4.03)
- The Chosen are Blood Elves — revealed by The Speaker's monologue and physical transformation (session 1.5.1/1.6.5)
- The Speaker is dead (killed by party, session 1.7.3)
- Ygritte was abducted by Blood Elves as the resurrection vessel — she is of Heimdall's bloodline
- Plans found on the Speaker: resurrection ritual in 5 nights at midnight
- Hannah's tomb in the Thrandurassil catacombs was empty — her corpse is gone
- Hannah's entry on the Thrandurassil family tree mural was concealed with magic; Caerdan is her half-brother
- Every night creatures attack Hammerfall from below; at dawn they retreat

## What They Don't Know (DM)
- Hannah is the daughter of Heimdall (a god)
- Hannah is Hannah the Forsaken — a demigod servant of Osgilliath, lord of the 3rd tier of Hel
- The Chosen are Blood Elves — fiends in elven form
- The resurrection requires a vessel from Heimdall's bloodline — current target is Ygritte
- Osgilliath is behind everything; Hannah is his direct contact point in the mortal world

## Session History
- **1.2.0**: Hannah's soul freed from Elven demi-plane prison. Party destroyed the ruby portal.
- **1.4.03**: Heimdall visits the Black Tide Tavern — drawn by Hannah's presence. Told party Hannah is his daughter; mother is High Queen Aduni. Implored them to save her.
- **1.5.1**: The Speaker monologues — reveals Blood Elf history, confirms Ygritte as the resurrection vessel, looks pointedly at Ivar. Raises claws. Blood Elves attack the council chambers.
- **1.5.1**: Ygritte abducted by Blood Elves during the chaos. Dragged into the Pit.
- **1.6.1**: Party pursues captors. Find the Thrandurassil family tree mural — Hannah's entry concealed. The Speaker seals them in.
- **1.6.2**: Tomb of the Lost — Hannah's sarcophagus is empty. Babau demon escapes.
- **1.6.3**: Party discovers the sealed door with 8 god symbols. Veylith identifies the Pilgrimage of the Eight.
- **1.6.4–1.6.5**: Njord temple completed (4/8 runes).
- **1.7.3**: The Speaker killed. Party level 7. Plans confirm: 5 nights until midnight resurrection ritual.
