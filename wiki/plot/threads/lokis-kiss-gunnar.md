# Loki's Kiss — Gunnar's Curse
*Gunnar wears a ring bearing Loki's influence; minor effects so far, but significant potential*

## Status
**Dormant** — effects present but not yet a party focus. Hannah's resurrection is the current priority.

## What the Players Know
- Gunnar took the ring from Brelka's body
- The ring is called Loki's Kiss
- Loki has been present as a watcher during Gunnar's visions (Midsommerblót)
- Loki has spoken directly to Gunnar twice — Temple of Tyr entrance, and during the Hrimgaldr hunt (provided directions to the Gods' Glade)
- Gunnar can transform into a rat at will (minor ability)
- The ring is cursed

## What They Don't Know (DM)
- The ring is a direct channel to Loki — his presence and contacts are intentional, not incidental
- Loki used the ring to drive Brelka to betray Loki's Hand — a joke at his own faction's expense
- Loki is building toward an offer: expanded transformation powers (potentially combat forms) in exchange for acts of betrayal, trickery, or character-shaking consequence
- The rat transformation may be just the beginning
- Gunnar's supernatural mirror and the ring are two divine threads on one character — potential for conflict

## Key Players
- [[Gunnar]] — wearing the ring; receiving visions
- [[Loki]] — the intelligence behind the ring; agenda TBD
- [[Brelka]] — previous wearer; killed by the party; her lycanthropy may have left a residue on the ring

## Key Locations
- Wherever Gunnar is

## Session History
- **1.2.8** "Loki's Kiss": Party killed Brelka. Gunnar took the ring.
- **1.4.01** "Midsommerblót": Loki present as a cheeky observer in Gunnar's visions during the blót. Watching, not acting.
- **1.7.3** "Afternight": Loki made direct contact with Gunnar at the Temple of Tyr entrance. Wry and amused — commented on Gunnar's half-elven blood opening the door. The scale briefly appeared cracked as he spoke.
- **Hrimgaldr hunt** (session TBC): Loki contacted Gunnar again, providing information on how to find the Gods' Glade and the beast Hrimgaldr within it.

## Next Steps / DM Plans
This thread can be escalated when Hannah's arc creates space or when the party actively investigates the ring.

Escalation options:
- Loki's visions become more urgent or disturbing
- The rat transformation grows in scope (longer duration, full wererat risk?)
- Loki makes direct contact — offers a bargain or sets a task
- The ring's curse deepens: Gunnar begins showing Brelka-like symptoms (paranoia, aggression)
- The party seeks to remove the ring — requires a quest (god favour, specific ritual, Loki's permission)

Tonally: Loki is a trickster, not purely evil. His presence so far has been observer → helpful informant → next step is tempter. The arc of his engagement with Gunnar is escalating. The offer, when it comes, should feel almost reasonable — the cost only becomes clear in hindsight.

The bargain structure: expanded transformation (potentially combat forms) in exchange for betrayal, trickery, or acts that shake Gunnar's character. See [[lore/lokis-kiss|Loki's Kiss]] for the cost breakdown.

## Contradictions & Open Questions
- ✅ Loki's Temple of Tyr tone confirmed: wry, amused, observer not actor. Teasing Gunnar about his half-elven blood. Not threatening — watching.
- ❓ During the Hrimgaldr contact, was Loki's tone similar (helpful informant) or did it shift toward something more purposeful?
- ❓ Has Gunnar told the party about Loki's contacts, or is he keeping it to himself?
- ❓ Why Gunnar specifically — is it the ring (Loki's general instrument), Gunnar's orc/elf mixed heritage, his mirror, or something else?
- ❓ Is the rat transformation a ring feature or a Brelka lycanthropy residue?
