# The Guild
*Criminal-merchant empire — Pact seat held by Yennen*

## Overview
Predominantly Norse but pays no attention to race or creed — the Guild's power is coin, and coin attracts merchants, pirates, and mercenaries alike. Disputes are handled brutally and violently. A saying: *"The face may change, but the price does not."* Power within the Guild is fleeting; wealth and territory change hands constantly.

## Goals
Control trade and finance. Maximize profit. Currently expanding influence over Hammerfall through the Pact seat.

## Leadership
[[Yennen]] — current Guild representative in Hammerfall. A fat, amiable dwarf whose motivations are opaque.

True power: [[Fafnir]] — a dwarf corrupted by cursed gold, transformed into a dragon. The Guild's hidden master (or patron).

## Members
- [[Tarod]] — enforcer, from Murgrog
- "Pathogen" ([[Hanzt]]) — former alchemical interrogator, now a fugitive

## Resources & Influence
- Pact seat: taxes, moneylending, merchant licensing, dock trade, soldier pay
- Slave farming (orphanage operations — Riven's backstory)
- Influence via bribed Fingers within [[Loki's Hand]]

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

**Fafnir** is the Guild's dark secret at the top — a dwarf who was corrupted by cursed gold and transformed into a dragon, roughly analogous to Smaug but Norse. This is a season 2+ hook.

The Guild has bribed one of Kvarrac's Fingers in Loki's Hand — this is the invisible hand behind the Shallows power struggle.

The Guild ran an orphanage as a slave farm (Riven's backstory). This is one of their darker operations.
