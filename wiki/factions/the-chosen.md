# The Chosen
*Full name: The Chosen of Osgilliath — religious faction controlling the Catacombs*

## Overview
Members of [[The Pact]], controlling the Catacombs, temples, and religious life of Hammerfall. Publicly they are the city's religious authority; privately they are a cult devoted to the demon lord [[Osgilliath]].

## Goals
Resurrect [[Hannah]] the Forsaken into a new corporeal form so she can lead them and serve as direct contact with Osgilliath.

## Leadership
[[The Speaker]] — Pact representative. A Blood Elf. **Deceased** — killed by party in the council chambers, session 1.7.3.

Leadership structure after the Speaker's death is unknown — the Chosen operate as a collective. Someone or something is still executing the ritual.

## Resources & Influence
- Full control of the Catacombs and all eight temples (Pilgrimage of the Eight)
- Religious authority over sacrifices and worship city-wide
- Pact seat legitimizes their operations

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

The Chosen are Blood Elves — transformed by Hannah's ritual, feeding from her power. Not merely cultists but fiends in elven form.

The Speaker's monologue confirmed the full history (session 1.5.1/1.6.5): he was one of the original cultists who drank Hannah's blood. He is centuries old. He confirmed Ygritte as the vessel, revealed himself, and attacked.

Every night since the reveal, creatures pour from the catacombs into Hammerfall. They take people below. At dawn they retreat. This is the Blood Elves feeding and expanding their forces.

Resurrection ritual: midnight, 5 nights from session 1.7.3.
