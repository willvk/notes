# The Pact
*The governing body of Hammerfall — also the name of the original alliance that conquered it*

## Overview
The Pact is both the agreement that enabled the conquest of Hammerfall and the governing body that resulted from it. Four factions hold seats; each controls distinct city functions.

## Members & Duties

| Representative | Faction | Duties |
|---------------|---------|--------|
| [[Ivar]] | [[Norse]] | Law, food allocation, property, trials, punishment, maintenance, management of Elven population and artifacts |
| [[Orgrim]] | [[Ork\|Orks]] | Guards, city defense, food distribution to Elves, trial by combat, smithing |
| [[Yennen]] | [[The Guild]] | Taxes, moneylending, merchant licensing, dock trade, soldier pay, city maintenance |
| [[The Speaker]] | [[The Chosen]] | Catacombs, temples, religious events, worship, sacrifices |

## Goals
Maintain control of Hammerfall and its Elven population. Each member faction pursues its own interests within this framework.

## Relationships
| Faction | Stance |
|---------|--------|
| [[Elves]] | Subjugated — managed by Ivar's seat |
| [[Loki's Hand]] | Tolerated criminal element in the Shallows |
| [[The Chosen]] | Internal — The Speaker sits on the Pact; their agenda is concealed |

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

Season 1 last plot beat: "The Pact is Broken — someone has betrayed the Pact." This betrayal is the season finale hook.
