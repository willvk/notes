# Loki's Hand
*Criminal smuggling network — controls the Shallows underworld*

## Overview
The dominant criminal organisation in [[The Shallows]]. Controls smuggling, the black market, and the Undercroft beneath [[Mongeet]]. Named after Loki — the trickster god favours the cunning, and this network operates through misdirection and silence.

## Goals
Control of Shallows trade and contraband. Currently: survival and quiet expansion under Kvarrac's cautious leadership.

## Leadership — New Structure
**Kvarrac** — dead (killed by Brelka, session 1.2.4)
**Brelka** — dead (killed by party, session 1.2.8)

**New Fingers (as of session 1.2.5):** Varka proposed rebuilding the Hand with the party. The five new Fingers are:
- [[characters/varka|Varka]] — founding member, tavern base of operations
- [[characters/pcs|Gunnar]] (PC)
- [[characters/pcs|Riven]] (PC)
- [[characters/pcs|Brawn]] (PC)
- [[characters/pcs|Hanzt]] (PC)

Loki's Hand is severely weakened after the coup and its aftermath. Influence must be rebuilt from scratch.

## Resources & Influence
- The Undercroft — a network of tunnels turned smuggler's haven beneath Mongeet
- Black-market food distribution in the Shallows
- Connections to dock smugglers and the Black Tide Tavern

## DM Notes
> Private — not on website.

Internal fracture: Brelka (wererat, disease-driven rage) is building a coup. The Guild's bribed Finger is an accelerant. Kvarrac is focused on keeping the peace and feeding the Shallows, not picking fights.

The Loki's Hand power struggle is the Catalyst plot threat — a Shallows boss confronting the party with tied PC backstory themes.
