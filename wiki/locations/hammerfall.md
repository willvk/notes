# Hammerfall
*aka Dres Elas (former Elven name) — The primary city of Act 1*

## Overview
Named by [[The Pact]] when conquered in 2790 BCE from its Elven population. Built on a natural harbour and rock formation, protected on all sides. Said to be protected by impenetrable Elven magics.

Divided into two areas with strictly controlled access between them: [[Upper Hammerfall]] and [[The Shallows]].

Total population: approximately 10,000.

## Districts / Areas

### [[Upper Hammerfall]]
The former Elven noble quarter. Now the seat of Pact power. Was found abandoned and eerily preserved when the party emerged from the Catacombs — a city suspended in time, the Elves having vanished instantly. (See session 1.2.0.)

### [[The Shallows]]
The commoner quarter. Population ~6,000. Divided into:
- **Njord's Mercy** — tower, chapel, and river port district
- **[[Mongeet]]** — former Fishmonger's Meet; party base of operations
- **Eastern Elf Shallows** — Elven ghetto
- **Western Elf Shallows** — skilled Elven labourers, slightly higher class
- **Richeri** — market district, road to Upper Hammerfall (the Kingsroad)
- **Orgate** — southwestern district, former Ork gate

### [[Catacombs]]
Below the city. Elven-built pilgrimage temples to the 8 major gods. Controlled by [[The Chosen]].

## Factions Present
- [[The Pact]] — governing body (Ivar/Norse, Orgrim/Orks, Yennen/Guild, The Speaker/Chosen)
- [[Loki's Hand]] — Shallows criminal network
- [[The Chosen]] — Catacombs and temples
- Elven population — subjugated, managed by Ivar
