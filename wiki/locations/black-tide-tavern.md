# The Black Tide Tavern
*Party base of operations — Mongeet, The Shallows*

## Overview
A tavern in [[Mongeet]] built on stilts over the river. Run by [[Varka]] "Low Tide" Brogg. Permits smugglers to operate under three rules: no killing, no cheating the house, no slitting throats where she drinks.

## Key Characters
- [[Varka]] — proprietor
- [[Balder]] (PC) — works here for money to fund his drinking
- [[Heimdall]] — visited here when drawn to Hannah's presence re-entering the world

## Notable Features
- River access — docks nearby
- Smuggler meeting point for [[Loki's Hand]]-adjacent operations
- Tavern sits above the river on stilts

## DM Notes
> Private.

Three hidden secrets about this location:

1. **Ghost Ship Signal** — on foggy nights the tavern lanterns flicker in a specific pattern, signalling smugglers to meet at the docks. The tavern is an active signal post for illicit trade.

2. **River Monster's Tribute** — every full moon, a mysterious offering (bloody fish, trinkets, coins) appears at the base of the stilts. Something beneath the river is being appeased. Connects to Mohr's river monster sighting (something with too many eyes).

3. **Hidden Trapdoor** — behind the bar under a keg. Leads to an underground cavern that goes much deeper than storage. Potential Catacombs connection.
