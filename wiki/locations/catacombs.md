# The Catacombs
*aka The Pilgrimage of the Eight — Elven-built temple complex beneath Hammerfall*

## Overview
Watery caves and temples to the 8 major gods, built by the Elves as a sacred pilgrimage route. The Elves called it the Pilgrimage of the Eight. Now controlled (outer sections) by [[The Chosen]], though their power has dwindled in the deeper sections.

## Entrances
- The Black Tide Tavern / Smugglers Cave
- Eastern Elf Shallows
- Richeri
- Caves southeast of Hammerfall (party introduction point)

## Depth 1
- **Loki Temple** — Loki's Hand operate here
- **Heimdall Temple** — Barghest and goblins
- **Upper Hammerfall** — Elven royal tombs
- **Elven Shallows** — Heimdall's Temple / disruptions in the earth
- **Smugglers Cave**

## Depth 2
- **Njord Temple** — water temple; iron boots, water breathing / blessing / water spirit
- **Frigg Temple** — temple of fate
- **Tyr Temple** — justice and sacrifice temple

## Depth 3
- **Odin Temple**
- **Thor Temple**
- **Freya Temple** — entrance via Richeri

## Key Characters
- [[The Chosen]] — control outer sections, power dwindling deeper in
- [[Loki's Hand]] — operate in and around the Loki Temple

## DM Notes
> Private.

The royal elven tombs in Depth 1 (Upper Hammerfall section) are significant — this is where the party found Hannah's prison portal in session 1.2.0.

Each temple depth increases in divine power and complexity of encounter. The Tyr temple (1.7.4) features the Judgment Construct — a shifting-law boss encounter.
