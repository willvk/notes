# Heimdall's Bloodline
*Heimdall travelled widely and fathered many descendants — shaping fortunes, castes, and bloodlines across Valkair*

## Overview
In Norse myth, Heimdall is credited with giving rise to the caste system by travelling among mortals and fathering children in each social stratum. Valkair mirrors this: Heimdall journeyed across the known world, bedding many peoples and leaving behind a scattered lineage of descendants. These descendants carry a diluted divine inheritance — the degree of power it confers has not yet been quantified, but the bloodline is real and significant.

## Known Descendants
- **[[Hannah]]** — direct daughter of Heimdall and Queen Aduni of Thrandurassil. Demigod. The most powerful known descendant, enhanced further by Osgilliath's gift.
- **[[Ygritte]]** — confirmed carrier of Heimdall's bloodline. Currently wife of [[Ivar]], formerly of [[Balder]].
- **Many others** — Heimdall's travels were extensive. There are likely significant numbers of bloodline descendants across different peoples and social classes throughout Valkair. Identities largely unknown.

## Significance
The Chosen require a body from Heimdall's bloodline to serve as Hannah's resurrection vessel — the bloodline provides sufficient metaphysical "weight" to anchor a demigod soul. Ygritte is their current target.

The diluted divine inheritance is a potential story development thread. Descendants may exhibit minor gifts — heightened perception, unusual luck, or susceptibility to divine influence — but this has not been formalised.

## Story Potential
- Locating other bloodline descendants could be a resource (allies, vessels, power sources) or a threat (The Chosen hunting them)
- The breadth of the bloodline across castes echoes Heimdall's myth directly — a divine fingerprint across all levels of Valkair society
- Act 2+ potential: discovering the scope of the bloodline, what it means, whether Heimdall is aware

## Contradictions & Open Questions
- ❓ Does Heimdall know how many descendants he has? Does he keep track?
- ❓ Are there other individuals in the current cast who unknowingly carry the bloodline?
