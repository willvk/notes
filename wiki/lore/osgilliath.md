# Osgilliath
*aka Osgilliath the Destroyer — Pit Lord of Hel, 3rd tier*

## Overview
A Pit Fiend and lord of the 3rd tier of Hel. Patron of [[Hannah]] the Forsaken and the power behind [[The Chosen]].

## Role in Valkair
Osgilliath's cult found Hannah when she had amassed her following of cutthroats — a small number of servitors who sought her out, promising her the power she desired.

Hannah underwent a ritual in the depths of the labyrinth beneath Dres Elas — removing her arms at the elbow, crucified on an inverted cross within a devil's circle. Osgilliath's spirit flowed into her, regrowing her arms as fiendish claws and reshaping her into his blessed servant.

Hannah then shared the "blessing" with her cultists — opening her veins to them. Those who drank her blood were transformed into Blood Elves (claws, forked tongues, or swollen size) filled with killing frenzy. They feed by returning to Hannah.
