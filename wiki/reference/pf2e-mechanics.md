## Key Conditions

### Frightened [value]
- Applies a status penalty equal to its value to **all checks and DCs**.
- Decreases by 1 at the **end of each of the affected creature's turns** unless specified otherwise.
- Always write with a value: Frightened 1, Frightened 2, etc.
- Frightened 2 = −2 penalty, lasts 2 turns naturally.

### Grabbed
- Applies **Off-Guard** (formerly flat-footed — use "off-guard" in Remaster) and **Immobilized**.
- Manipulate actions while Grabbed require a DC 5 flat check or they're lost.
- Applied by a **successful** Grapple action.

### Restrained
- Applies **Off-Guard** and **Immobilized**.
- Cannot use **any** attack or manipulate actions except to Escape or Force Open bonds.
- Applied by a **critical success** on a Grapple action, or by specific abilities.
- To escape: Escape action (Athletics or Acrobatics vs. the restrainer's Athletics DC or ability DC).

### Slowed [value]
- Reduces actions regained each turn by the value.
- Slowed 1 = lose 1 action per turn.

## Grapple Action (Athletics)
- **1 action.** Requires a free hand (unless you have the Grapple weapon trait).
- Check: **Athletics** vs. target's **Fortitude DC**.
- **Critical Success:** target is **Restrained** until end of your next turn (or until you move or they Escape).
- **Success:** target is **Grabbed** until end of your next turn (or until you move or they Escape).
- **Failure:** no effect; existing Grabbed/Restrained ends.
- **Critical Failure:** if target was Grabbed/Restrained, they break free and may grab you or knock you prone.
- Target must be no more than one size larger than you (unless already Grabbed/Restrained).

## Bonus Types (no "advantage" in PF2e)

PF2e does not have "advantage." Instead use typed bonuses — only the highest of each type applies.

| Type | When to use |
|------|-------------|
| **Circumstance bonus** | Positioning, tools, specific situation |
| **Status bonus** | Spells, conditions, magical boons |
| **Item bonus** | Magic items |
| **Untyped** | Rare; stacks with everything but not itself |

**For divine boons / saves:** use **+2 status bonus** to the relevant save(s) against the relevant trait(s).
Example: *+2 status bonus to Will saves against [fear] and [mental] trait effects.*

## Weapon Traits (relevant)

**Grapple** — can use this weapon for the Grapple action even without a free hand. The action has the weapon trait.

**Finesse** — can use Dexterity instead of Strength for attack rolls.

**Agile** — the multiple attack penalty is −4/−8 instead of −5/−10.

## Frequency / Duration Language

- Use **"once per day (recharges at daily preparations)"** not "once per long rest."
- "Until the end of your next turn" = approximately 1 round.
- "For 1 minute" = 10 rounds (combat encounter).

## Notes on Item DCs

Magic items use the same level-based DC table above. A level 7 item/boon sets save DCs at **23**. When creating custom divine boons:
- Match item level to party level for balanced challenge.
- Level 7 party → DC 23 for all saves vs. boon effects.
